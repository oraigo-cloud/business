# Corporate Training Proposals

This folder contains ready-to-use markdown templates for different training request scenarios.

---

## 1. Corporate Training Request (In-person)

```markdown
# Corporate Training Proposal - In-Person

**Client:** [Client Name]  
**Date:** [Date]  
**Prepared By:** EBT Tech Labs

## Executive Summary
Brief overview of the training request and objectives.

## Training Objectives
- Objective 1
- Objective 2
- Objective 3

## Training Modules / Curriculum
1. Module 1: [Description]
2. Module 2: [Description]
3. Module 3: [Description]

## Timeline & Location
- Start Date: [Date]
- End Date: [Date]
- Location: [City/On-site]

## Cost & Payment Terms
- Total Cost: [Amount]
- Payment Terms: [Details]

## Contact & Next Steps
- Contact Person: [Name, Email, Phone]
- Call to action for client to proceed
```
