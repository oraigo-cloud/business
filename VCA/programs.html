Programs

Yoga for Balance

A progressive 12-week track that emphasizes balance, mobility, and breath. Each class includes warm-up mobility, standing sequences, gentle seated work, and guided relaxation.

  • Beginner-friendly progressions
  • Chair and mat options
  • Focus on joint health and posture

Martial Arts Basics

Low-impact movement drills drawn from traditional forms. Emphasis on stance, balance, controlled striking mechanics (air-based), and simple partner drills for coordination.

  • Non-contact, safety-first instruction
  • Designed to improve gait, reaction time, and confidence
  • Adaptations for joint limitations

Wellness Workshops

Monthly workshops on breathing, sleep hygiene, nutrition for midlife, injury prevention and managing aches through movement.

Workshops can be added to any membership or booked separately.
